Appetizer
A small dish served before the main meal to stimulate the appetite, such as soup or salad.
Main Course
The primary dish of a meal, usually containing protein like meat, fish, or a vegetarian option with sides.
Dessert
A sweet course served at the end of the meal, such as cake, ice cream, or fruit.
Snack
A light portion of food eaten between meals, often quick and easy to prepare.
Beverage
Any type of drink, including water, juice, soda, tea, or coffee.
